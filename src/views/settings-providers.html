How OAuth delegation works

This local node (id.ai.on) cannot receive OAuth callbacks from Google, GitHub, or Discord because external providers cannot reach a local URL.

When you click "Connect" on the Connections page, a popup opens on Hive-ID where you complete the OAuth flow. Once authorized, your tokens are delivered back to this node via the handoff mechanism and stored encrypted in the local database.

Token channels (Telegram, Discord bot, Signal, WhatsApp) are entered directly in the Channel Setup wizard and do not require Hive-ID.
